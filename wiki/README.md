# Wiki index

An LLM-maintained wiki on Real Zaragoza CF — see the root `README.md` for the project's intent and `.claude/CLAUDE.md` for the conventions these pages follow. Sources of truth: [realzaragoza.com](https://www.realzaragoza.com/) and [Spanish Wikipedia](https://es.wikipedia.org/wiki/Real_Zaragoza); every page says so up top and flags anything that couldn't be checked against them.

## Institution

- [`current-situation.md`](current-situation.md) — ownership, board, sporting/coaching staff, and the stadium situation, as of today. The most volatile page in the wiki right now.
- [`finances.md`](finances.md) — debt history (the 2011 insolvency and its long tail), ownership eras by financial character, the 2026 capital increase, wage caps, stadium sponsorship.
- [`identity-fan-culture.md`](identity-fan-culture.md) — nicknames, crest/colours, the derbi aragonés rivalry, ultras, anthem, socios.

## History

- [`history.md`](history.md) — founding, seasons played, honours, notable eras, relegations, stadium history.
- [`records.md`](records.md) — all-time top scorer, appearances, Pichichi winners, biggest results, legendary players by era.

## Football

- [`squad.md`](squad.md) — current (2026–27 preseason) squad rebuild: departures, signings, market priorities. Goes stale fast by design — re-check often during the transfer window.
- [`academy.md`](academy.md) — youth structure, the 2026 academy overhaul, historical and current graduates.
